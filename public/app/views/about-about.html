About Us

IndiaReads.com started with a vision to provide one-stop affordable reading across India. Today, it is India's largest book rental platform, with services in 500+ cities. We offer over 1.8 lac titles to choose from across genres like college textbooks, competitive exams, fiction, non-fiction, self-help, technology, management.

IndiaReads.com corporate reading patform leverages tech to get employees hooked on-to a very productive habit i.e. reading. On this company branded platform, employees can cultivate reading habits & refer professional recommendations for capability building.

We believe that all our reading needs are mostly time-bound and renting reading resources is a cost-efficient & greener alternative. Why buy, if you can just read & return ? #IndiaKaLibrary
